FAQS

What is the difference between Aadhaar linked and DBT-enabled Aadhaar seeded bank accounts? Aadhaar linked: Aadhaar is connected for KYC only. DBT enabled: Aadhaar is seeded in NPCI for receiving government benefits directly.

How do I check if my account is DBT enabled? You can check via your bank branch, NPCI portal, or UIDAI website.

Why didn't I receive my scholarship even though my account is Aadhaar linked? Because only linking is not enough, your account must be DBT-enabled (NPCI seeded).

What should I do if my DBT is going to the wrong bank account? Visit your bank and update your Aadhaar seeding request to map the correct account

Is Aadhaar seeding a one-time process? Yes, but if you change banks or accounts, you must update your Aadhaar seeding again
